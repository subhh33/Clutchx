Our Story

BUILT BY RIDERS,
FOR RIDERS.

WE WERE STUCK ON A
HIGHWAY AT 2AM.

Our founder's Royal Enfield died on NH44, 60km from the nearest town. No way to alert other riders. No way to find help. Just WhatsApp groups that nobody checked at that hour.

That night, ClutchX was born. The idea that India's 230 million riders deserved something better than scattered group chats and borrowed Instagram pages.

Two years later, we're building the platform we wish had existed — a real moto-social network, built India-first, for the Indian riding spirit.

Legal

PRIVACY POLICY.

Last updated: March 2025 | Effective: March 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

ClutchX collects information to provide and improve our services. This includes location data (GPS coordinates when the app is active), profile information you provide, ride statistics and route data, and device information.

2. Location Data

We collect precise GPS data to power the live rider map and SOS features. You can control your visibility settings at any time, including enabling ghost mode to ride privately. Location data is never sold to third parties.

3. SOS Emergency Data

When SOS is activated, your location is broadcast to nearby riders and your pre-designated emergency contacts. This data is used solely for emergency response purposes and deleted after 48 hours.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Power the live rider map and nearby rider discovery
  • Enable SOS emergency broadcasts
  • Provide trip planning and route features
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Send relevant notifications (which you can disable)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Anonymised, aggregated data may be shared with research partners. Emergency data is shared only as required for emergency response.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, email privacy@clutchx.in. Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at dpo@clutchx.in.
